Introduction

Every Monk players has unique features, traits and behaviour which differentiate them from one another. These features which includes playing agility, dribbling, intelligence and so many others and these features are locked individually to every Monk player, additional features can be purchased and added to each Monk player.

Over the years, the football community and enthusiasts have been in search a decentralized ecosystem, cardano football league gives the fans the full entitlement to vote for the best monk player of the year as well as other key medals. Owning a player NFT gives the holders right to governance and decision making, this means the higher the number of player Nft a holders holds, the higher voting rights.

A team is said to be fully saturated when it has a total number of 20 monk players in it while only 11 players from each team is allowed to play on the football pitch
